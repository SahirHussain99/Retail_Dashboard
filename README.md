# Toys Co. Executive Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This repository contains a comprehensive, single-page Power BI Executive Dashboard designed for a toy retail company operating across multiple cities. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into high-level, actionable business intelligence, tracking **$14.44M in total revenue** and **1.09M in total units sold**.

The goal of this project is to provide stakeholders with immediate clarity on product performance, geographic trends, and seasonal traffic to optimize inventory allocation and marketing spend.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools
* **BI Tool:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Sources:** Products, Stores, Sales, Inventory, and Calendar tables
* **Data Modeling:** Star Schema design with optimized relationships
* **DAX:** Utilized for advanced Time Intelligence (Normalized Year-over-Year pacing calculations) and dynamic KPIs

## 📈 Key Features & Insights Delivered
* **Executive Summary Banner:** Instant visibility into core business health metrics: Total Revenue ($14.44M), Units Sold (1.09M), and normalized YoY pacing metrics.
* **Product Tiering Analysis:** Clear isolation of top-performing items (Lego Bricks at $2.39M) versus underperforming dead stock (Uno Card Game at $21.65K) to guide inventory purchasing.
* **Regional & Foot Traffic Profiling:** Granular breakdown proving that Downtown location types drive the highest sales volume (73K units in Toys), providing a blueprint for future retail expansion.
* **Interactive Exploration:** Integrated Year-over-Year toggle buttons (2022 vs. 2023) and City-specific dropdown slicers for real-time data drilling.


## 🎯 Business Impact
By deploying this dashboard, stakeholders can successfully shift from reactive tracking to proactive strategy safely reducing capital tied up in low-velocity products and focusing supply chain resources on high-demand urban centers.
